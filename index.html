Born in Virginia, Bruce began working in construction during the early restoration efforts in historical downtown Fredericksburg. For the past 20 years, he ran projects in both the United States and The Bahamas.

Bruce's experience is vast: Small tenant improvement projects in commercial space; 13,000 square foot single family custom homes; 700 acre private island sustainability projects.

Notable clients have ranged from Grandma Mac to Shakira.
